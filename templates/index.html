Who We Are?

DMC MARKETS, a licensed, Corporate Member of Pakistan Mercantile Exchange. We have always followed a client centric approach and placed our clients needs at the center of our operations. It is our aim to remain among the best online brokers in Pakistan and to retain our reputation as a most reliable and trusted broker.

At DMC, we are dedicated to providing our clients with the latest in trading technology and tools through continuous investments in innovation. In addition to new instruments, we regularly update our offering with new platforms and services to meet the varying needs of traders, while honoring our commitment to transparency and fair trading practices. We also offer comprehensive educational material, to help traders learn about online trading and the financial markets. Our award-winning customer support team is on hand, 24/5, to assist with any issues you may encounter or questions you may have.
